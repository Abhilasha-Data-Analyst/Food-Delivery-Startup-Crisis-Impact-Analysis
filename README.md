# **🥗Food Delivery Startup Crisis Impact Analysis💻📈**



## **1. About Project📌**



QuickBite Express is a food delivery startup that connects customers with nearby restaurants and cloud kitchens. In June 2025, the company faced a major crisis that impacted orders, revenue, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.



This project analyzes the impact by comparing **Pre-Crisis (Jan–May 2025)** and **Crisis Phase (Jun–Sep 2025)** performance. The objective is to identify the key drivers behind business decline and provide recommendations for recovery planning.

## **9. Data Driven Insights**



### 📉 **1. Business Collapse Overview**



The crisis caused a severe decline across all major business metrics:

* Orders declined by **70.9%**
* Revenue declined by **71%**
* Customer demand and trust dropped significantly



👉 This indicates a system-wide business failure affecting both demand and experience.



### **🚚 2. Delivery Performance Breakdown**



Delivery delays increased sharply during the crisis:

* Pre-crisis: **37 min → 39 min (+2 min delay)**
* Crisis: **42 min → 60 min (+17 min delay)**



👉 Even small delivery delays directly impacted customer satisfaction and repeat orders.

#### 

### **⭐ 3. Customer Experience Decline**



Major customer complaints included: Food Quality, Food Safety, Stale Food, Packaging Issues, Bad Taste, Very Late Delivery

👉 These issues led to:

* Lower ratings
* Reduced trust
* Increased cancellations



### **👥 4. Loyalty \& Retention Impact**



* 80% of loyal customers stopped ordering
* Even high-rating customers (>4.5) disengaged



👉 This shows that operational issues damaged long-term customer trust.

#### 

### **💰 5. Revenue Impact Chain**



Revenue decline followed a clear chain reaction:👉 Delivery delays increased → Customer satisfaction dropped → Ratings fell → Orders declined → Revenue decreased



Final impact:

* Revenue dropped from **₹37.6M → ₹10.9M**
* Total loss: **₹26.7M (−71%)**

#### 

### **🏙️ 6. City-Level Impact**



High-value Tier-1 cities were most affected:

* Bengaluru
* Mumbai
* Delhi



👉 These cities contributed the highest revenue, amplifying the overall business loss.



### **🍽️ 7. Restaurant Ecosystem Impact**



* Restaurant performance declined by 71% – 92%
* Both restaurants and cloud kitchens were impacted



👉 The crisis affected the entire supply ecosystem.



## **10. Project Outcome**



### **Key Findings**

* Order decline: **(−70.9%)**
* Revenue decline: **(−71%)**
* Delivery SLA deterioration: **+17 min delay**
* Customer churn: **80%**



### **Business Impact**

* Severe operational breakdown
* Loss of customer trust
* High revenue contraction



## **11. Business Recommendations**



### 🚚 **Operational Improvements**

* Reduce delivery delays
* Monitor delivery partner performance



### 🍔 **Food Quality Strategy**

* Food safety checks
* Packaging quality audits
* Restaurant quality scoring



### 👥 **Customer Retention Strategy**

* Loyalty programs for high-value customers
* Re-engagement of churned users



### 💰 **Revenue Recovery Strategy**

* Focus on Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi
* Focus on North Indian, South Indian, Biryani cuisines



### **🥗 Product Expansion**

* Introduce healthier food options for health-conscious users
